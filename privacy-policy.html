🔒 Privacy Policy

Navigate Germany — shaistashabbir-prog.github.io/navigate-germany

Last updated: 13 June 2026

Summary: Navigate Germany is a free information website. We do not sell your personal data. We use Google Analytics to understand how many people visit the site. We use partner referral links (Commerzbank, Advanzia, Wise) which may set cookies when you click them.

1. Who we are

Navigate Germany ("we", "our", "the site") is a free public resource providing guidance for immigrants living in Germany. The site is operated by Shaista Shabbir, Research Associate at TU Dortmund University / Lamarr Institute.

Contact: github.com/ShaistaShabbir-prog

2. What data we collect

2.1 Data you provide voluntarily

This site does not have registration forms, login systems or contact forms. If you contact us via WhatsApp or GitHub, you provide data voluntarily and those platforms' own privacy policies apply.

2.3 Local storage (your device only)

We store the following data in your browser's localStorage — it never leaves your device:

  • ng_lang — your chosen language preference
  • ng_ref_idx — which partner recommendation you last viewed
  • ng_ref_gone — whether you dismissed the recommendation widget
  • ng_saved — which guides you saved (heart button)

3. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to count visitors and understand which content is most helpful. Google Analytics collects anonymised usage data. We have enabled IP anonymisation.

Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Opt out of Google Analytics: tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

4. Google AdSense

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site or other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising at google.com/settings/ads.

Google's advertising privacy policy: policies.google.com/technologies/ads

5. Partner referral links

This site contains referral links to third-party financial services. When you click these links, the third party may set cookies on your device. We receive a small referral reward if you sign up through our links — this does not affect the price you pay or the product you receive.

6. Cookies

Navigate Germany itself does not set cookies. Cookies may be set by:

  • Google Analytics (tracking cookies, anonymised)
  • Google AdSense (advertising cookies)
  • Partner sites when you click referral links

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not affect your ability to use this site.

7. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right of access — request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to object — object to processing of your data for direct marketing
  • Right to portability — receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to lodge a complaint — with the relevant Data Protection Authority

As this site does not collect personal data beyond anonymised analytics, most of these rights apply to data held by Google Analytics rather than by us directly. Contact Google at support.google.com to exercise rights over Analytics data.

8. Children

This site is intended for adults (18+) seeking immigration information. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. External links

Navigate Germany links to official German government websites (bundesland portals, BAMF, Arbeitsagentur) and third-party resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We recommend reading their privacy policies before submitting any personal data.

10. Data retention

We do not store personal data on our servers. Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months per our Analytics configuration. Local storage data remains on your device until you clear your browser data.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page will reflect when it was last updated. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
