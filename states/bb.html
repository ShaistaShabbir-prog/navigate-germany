🏠 Living in Brandenburg

Cost of living

Average rent (1-bedroom): €800/month

  • Groceries: ~€200-300/month (ALDI, LIDL, REWE)
  • Health insurance (GKV): ~€200-400/month
  • Public transport monthly pass: ~€29 (Deutschlandticket)

🚌 Public Transport

💡 Deutschlandticket: €29/month — use on all regional transport across Germany
