About the platform

Clear guidance, official sources

Navigate Germany helps immigrants, students, families, refugees and new arrivals find the responsible German authority and understand the next practical step.

What we do

We organise public information into approachable state and life-module journeys.

What we do not do

We do not provide legal advice, make visa decisions, or replace qualified professionals and authorities.

Translation roadmap

Key homepage guidance is multilingual. Full page translations are being expanded and are clearly labelled.

Information can change. Every important decision should be verified with the linked official authority.
