Plain-language privacy

Your choices stay simple

Navigate Germany is a static information website. It has no user accounts, contact forms or server-side profile database.

Browser storage

Your selected language, saved guides and dismissed recommendation state may be stored locally on your device using localStorage. You can clear this through your browser.

Analytics status

No working analytics identifier is configured in this release. Placeholder tracking code does not identify a valid property and should not be treated as active analytics.

External websites

Official sources and optional partner links open third-party websites. Their privacy and cookie policies apply after you leave Navigate Germany.

Affiliate disclosure

Some clearly labelled links are partner or referral links. Shaista Shabbir may receive a reward if you choose to use one. These offers are optional, are not official German guidance and should be compared with alternatives.

Contact and data requests

Because the site does not operate accounts or a form database, it generally has no personal profile to retrieve or delete. For a privacy question, open an issue in the public project repository.
