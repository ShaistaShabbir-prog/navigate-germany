Journey roadmap

Skilled worker

Connect your employment, residence, registration and social-insurance steps.

First 7 days

  • Check your employment start documents.
  • Confirm accommodation and registration paperwork.
  • Keep visa and qualification records together.

First 30 days

  • Complete Anmeldung.
  • Confirm health and social insurance.
  • Check residence-permit or Blue Card follow-up.

First 90 days

  • Review payslips and tax class.
  • Understand probation and employment rights.
  • Plan family, language or recognition steps.

Documents to prepare

Passport, visa, employment contract, qualification and recognition records, health-insurance confirmation, rental records and authority correspondence.
