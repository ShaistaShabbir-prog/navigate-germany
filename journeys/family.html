Journey roadmap

Family in Germany

Prioritise residence, healthcare, childcare, school and local family support.

First 7 days

  • Keep family identity and relationship records accessible.
  • Confirm housing and health coverage.
  • Find urgent local family contacts.

First 30 days

  • Register family members.
  • Contact Kita, school or Schulamt early.
  • Check residence appointments and documents.

First 90 days

  • Ask the responsible office about family benefits.
  • Choose a pediatrician or family doctor.
  • Join language and family counselling services.

Documents to prepare

Passports, visas, birth and marriage certificates where relevant, certified translations if requested, registration and insurance records, school and vaccination records.
