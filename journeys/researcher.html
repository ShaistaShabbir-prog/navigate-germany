Journey roadmap

Researcher / PhD

Coordinate your host institution, funding, residence, insurance and local registration.

First 7 days

  • Meet your university or institute contact.
  • Confirm contract, scholarship or hosting documents.
  • Collect housing and registration records.

First 30 days

  • Complete Anmeldung and insurance steps.
  • Confirm residence requirements with the authority.
  • Set up institutional accounts and research access.

First 90 days

  • Review tax and social-insurance treatment.
  • Explore language and international-office support.
  • Plan family or long-term residence steps if relevant.

Documents to prepare

Passport and visa, hosting agreement or contract, funding proof, degree records, health insurance, housing documents and university correspondence.
