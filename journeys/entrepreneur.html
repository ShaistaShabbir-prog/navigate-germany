Journey roadmap

Entrepreneur

Start with residence permission, professional advice, registration and realistic financial planning.

First 7 days

  • Check whether your residence title permits self-employment.
  • Organise business-plan and funding records.
  • Identify local startup and chamber advice.

First 30 days

  • Clarify Gewerbe or freelance classification.
  • Arrange tax registration and bookkeeping advice.
  • Separate personal and business finances.

First 90 days

  • Review insurance and contractual risks.
  • Document invoices and expenses.
  • Check local permits and sector requirements.

Documents to prepare

Passport and residence documents, business plan, financing evidence, qualification records, registration documents, contracts and tax correspondence.
