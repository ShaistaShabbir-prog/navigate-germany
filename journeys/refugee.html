Journey roadmap

Refugee / asylum seeker

Use official procedures and qualified, independent counselling. This page does not assess asylum claims.

Immediate danger: call 112 for ambulance or fire, or 110 for police. For asylum and residence decisions, seek qualified counselling.

First 7 days

  • Follow official registration and accommodation instructions.
  • Keep every identity and authority document safe.
  • Ask for an interpreter or counselling support when needed.

First 30 days

  • Attend official appointments and record deadlines.
  • Ask about healthcare access and local social services.
  • Find independent migration or asylum counselling.

First 90 days

  • Check education, language and family support options.
  • Keep copies of all decisions and submissions.
  • Get legal advice promptly for a negative decision or deadline.

Documents to keep together

Identity documents, registration and arrival records, appointment notices, medical records, family documents, official decisions and proof of every submission.
