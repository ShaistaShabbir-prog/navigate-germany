Journey roadmap

International student

A calm first-90-days plan for enrolment, housing, insurance, residence and daily life.

First 7 days

  • Move in and request the landlord confirmation.
  • Complete university enrolment steps.
  • Confirm health-insurance status.

First 30 days

  • Register your address.
  • Open a suitable bank account.
  • Apply for or confirm your residence permit appointment.

First 90 days

  • Understand work-hour conditions on your permit.
  • Join language and student-support services.
  • Build a realistic monthly budget.

Documents to prepare

Passport, visa or residence document, admission and enrolment records, proof of funds, health-insurance confirmation, rental contract and Wohnungsgeberbestätigung.
