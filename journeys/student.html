Your roadmap

Follow these steps in order. Each step links to the relevant guide or official authority. Timelines are approximate.

Step-by-step roadmap

1
Apply to university
Submit application via uni-assist or directly. Prepare transcripts and language certificates.
→ 3–6 months before start
2
Apply for student visa
Visit German embassy/consulate with admission letter and blocked account proof.
→ 2–3 months before arrival
3
Open blocked account (Sperrkonto)
Fintiba or Expatrio. Deposit €934 × months of study to prove financial means.
→ Before visa appointment
4
Arrive in Germany
Book temporary accommodation (Studentenwohnheim or private).
Day 1
5
Register (Anmeldung)
Visit Bürgeramt within 2 weeks. Bring passport and tenancy agreement.
→ Week 1
6
Get health insurance
Public insurance via TK, AOK, or Barmer. Required for university enrolment.
→ Week 1
7
Enrol at university
Immatriculation with admission letter, insurance proof, and Anmeldebescheinigung.
→ Week 1–2
8
Open German bank account
Commerzbank, N26, or DKB. Need IBAN for scholarship payments.
→ Week 2
9
Get Aufenthaltserlaubnis
Apply for student residence permit at Ausländerbehörde with your university enrolment.
→ Month 1
10
Buy Deutschlandticket
€49/month unlimited local/regional transport across Germany.
→ Month 1
