Blue Card Quick Facts 2026

Official source: Make it in Germany ↗

✅ IT specialists: no degree with 3 of the past 7 years at graduate level
✅ Validity: 4 years
✅ Settlement permit: 21 months with B1 or 27 months with A1
✅ Family: immediate reunification

Chancenkarte Facts

Official source: Make it in Germany
✅ Come without job offer
✅ Valid: 1 year job search
✅ Part-time work: up to 20 hrs/week
✅ Trial work: 2 weeks allowed
✅ Finances required: €1,027/month
✅ Blocked account: €12,324 recommended
✅ After job: switch to Blue Card or Fachkräftevisum
⚠️ Not for EU citizens (already have work rights)
⚠️ No permanent residence from Chancenkarte alone
