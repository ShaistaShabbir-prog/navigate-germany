🏥 Finding a hospital (Krankenhaus)

In a life-threatening emergency, call 112. For non-life-threatening urgent care, go to the nearest Notaufnahme (A&E department) or call 116117.

KBV — Find a doctor/hospital

Official search for nearest open practices and hospitals by location and specialty.

🧠 Crisis and mental health support

You are not alone. Help is available 24/7 in Germany, in multiple languages, and always free.

Telefonseelsorge 0800 111 0 111

Crisis support, 24/7, free, anonymous. German and English.

→ telefonseelsorge.de

Nummer gegen Kummer

For children and parents: 116 111. Mon–Sat 14:00–20:00. Free.

→ nummergegenkummer.de

MIND Deutschland

Mental health support and resources in Germany.

→ mind-deutschland.de

Opfer-Telefon

Victim support: 116 006. For crime victims. 24/7. Free.

Hilfetelefon Gewalt

→ hilfetelefon.de

Addiction support. Free counselling online and by phone.
