🏠 Documents for Anmeldung (registration)

Anmeldung must be completed within 2 weeks of moving into permanent accommodation. You cannot register from a hotel.

Required documents

  • Valid passport or national ID card
  • Completed Anmeldeformular (registration form) — download from your city's website
  • Wohnungsgeberbestätigung — landlord confirmation letter (required since 2015)
  • Tenancy agreement (Mietvertrag) — sometimes accepted instead
Ask your landlord for the Wohnungsgeberbestätigung before your appointment — without it you cannot register.

What you receive

  • Anmeldebestätigung / Meldebescheinigung — your proof of residence. Guard it carefully — you will need it for bank accounts, insurance, employer, and many other services.
  • Steuer-ID — sent by post within 2–4 weeks

💡 Tip

Book your Anmeldung appointment before you move in — waiting times in Berlin and Munich can be 4–8 weeks. Many cities also offer online Anmeldung (check your city's website).

🏦 Documents for opening a bank account

Standard requirements

  • Valid passport or EU ID card
  • Meldebescheinigung (Anmeldung confirmation)
  • German phone number (for TAN/2FA)
  • German address

Without Meldebescheinigung (N26, Vivid)

  • Valid passport or EU ID
  • Video identification (VideoIdent) via app
  • German or international phone number
Traditional banks (Commerzbank, Deutsche Bank, Sparkasse) require Meldebescheinigung. Online banks (N26, Vivid) may accept you before Anmeldung.

🏥 Documents for health insurance (Krankenkasse)

If employed (GKV)

  • Valid passport or EU ID
  • Sozialversicherungsausweis (social security card — if you have one)
  • Employment contract or employer confirmation
  • Meldebescheinigung
  • IBAN (bank account)

Your employer registers you — give them your chosen Krankenkasse name on day 1. If you don't choose, the employer selects for you.

If self-employed or student (voluntary GKV)

  • Valid passport or EU ID
  • Meldebescheinigung
  • Income tax return or income proof
  • IBAN
  • If student: university enrolment certificate (Immatrikulationsbescheinigung)

TK — Techniker Krankenkasse

Largest GKV. Strong English support. Popular with international workers.

💼 Documents for job applications

Application documents (Bewerbungsmappe)

  • CV (Lebenslauf) in German format — tabular, photo included
  • Cover letter (Anschreiben) — mandatory in Germany
  • Degree/diploma certificates with certified German translation
  • University transcripts
  • Work references from previous employers (Arbeitszeugnisse)
  • Language certificates (Goethe, telc, DSH, TestDaF)

On day 1 at new employer

  • Valid passport or EU ID
  • Meldebescheinigung
  • Steuer-ID (11-digit tax ID)
  • Sozialversicherungsausweis (social security card)
  • Bank account IBAN for salary
  • Health insurance confirmation letter

🚗 Documents for driving licence exchange

Required documents

  • Valid passport or EU ID
  • Current foreign driving licence (original)
  • Certified German translation of licence (if not in Latin script)
  • Biometric passport photo (35×45mm)
  • Meldebescheinigung
  • Sehtest (eye test from any optician, ~€10)
  • Erste-Hilfe-Kurs certificate (first aid, 8 hours — if not already done)
  • Application form from Führerscheinstelle
  • Fee: ~€30–60 depending on state
Some countries have direct exchange agreements with Germany (no test required): USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Japan, Switzerland. Check the KBA database.
