🎓 Integration courses (Integrationskurs)

Official BAMF integration courses combine German language (A1-B1) with an orientation course (German values, law, history, culture).

Who is eligible: Immigrants with certain permit types, refugees, late resettlers.
Cost: Usually heavily subsidised (~€1.95/lesson). Free for eligible asylum seekers.
Duration: 700 hours language + 100 hours orientation.
Find a course: BAMF-NAvI course search

🤝 Free counselling & support

Migrationsberatung (MBE)

Free migration counselling for adults from third countries. BAMF-funded. In your language.

→ Find MBE counselling

Caritas

Catholic welfare organisation. Free social, migration and legal counselling. Nationwide.

→ caritas.de

AWO — Arbeiterwohlfahrt

Workers welfare association. Integration services, counselling, community programs.

→ awo.org

Diakonie

Protestant welfare organisation. Migration counselling and social support in many languages.

→ diakonie.de

IQ Network

Free counselling on qualification recognition for skilled migrants. Offices in all 16 states.

→ netzwerk-iq.de

Antidiskriminierungsstelle

Federal anti-discrimination agency. Free advice if you experience discrimination.
