⚡ Quick help

🔴 Kündigung received? You have 3 weeks to file Kündigungsschutzklage at Arbeitsgericht. It's free.
🔴 Official refusal (Bescheid)? You have 1 month to file Widerspruch in writing.

🛡️ Your rights

📋 Every official letter must state the authority and the legal basis
⏰ You always have a right to sufficient time to respond
🗣️ You have a right to an interpreter in legal proceedings
📁 You can request a copy of your file (Akteneinsicht)
🆓 Low-income residents can get a Beratungshilfeschein (€15 legal advice)
